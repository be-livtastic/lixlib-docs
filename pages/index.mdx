### What is Lix Library?

Lix Library is a suite of advanced, plug-and-play components built to **expand the capabilities of Wix** beyond its standard offerings. Each component in the library is designed to make your Wix website more dynamic and responsive, empowering you to deliver professional-grade features with minimal setup.

- **Customizable & Intuitive**: Tailor each component to match your brand, creating a cohesive and polished experience.

With Lix, you can give your site the interactive edge it needs, while still enjoying the user-friendly environment of Wix.
